Spider plant

The Spider plant (Chlorophytum comosum) is renowned for its adaptability and ease of growth, thriving in various conditions with minimal issues, mostly limited to brown tips. Named for its spider-like leaves or spiderettes, which hang down like spiders on a web, it comes in green or variegated varieties. The spiderettes often begin as small white flowers.
